Hearty Pasta

Hearty Pasta is a delicious and comforting dish that combines the flavors of rich tomato sauce, Italian sausage, and pasta. It's perfect for a satisfying dinner on a chilly evening.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 12 oz pasta of your choice
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the Italian sausage until browned, breaking it up into small pieces. Remove the sausage from the skillet and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for another 1 minute.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir in the cooked sausage. Bring the sauce to a simmer and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  5. Combine the cooked pasta and the sauce, stirring until the pasta is evenly coated. Serve with freshly grated Parmesan cheese. Enjoy!
