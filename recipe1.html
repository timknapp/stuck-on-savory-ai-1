Asparagus Delight

Asparagus Delight is a delicious and healthy dish that combines the flavors of fresh asparagus, garlic, and lemon. It's perfect for a light lunch or as a side dish for dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch of asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the asparagus, garlic, olive oil, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Toss well to coat the asparagus evenly.
  3. Arrange the asparagus on a baking sheet in a single layer. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the asparagus is tender but still crisp.
  5. Remove from the oven and serve immediately. Enjoy!
