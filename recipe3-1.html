Honey BBQ Chicken

Honey BBQ Chicken is a delicious grilled dish that combines the flavors of sweet honey and tangy barbecue sauce. It's perfect for a summer cookout or a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the honey, barbecue sauce, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, and black pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a large resealable plastic bag and pour in the marinade. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the marinade.
  4. Grill the chicken for 5-7 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) and the juices run clear.
  5. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
