Grilled Vegetable Skewers

Grilled Vegetable Skewers are a healthy and delicious side dish that can be easily customized with your favorite vegetables. They're perfect for summer cookouts and make a great addition to any meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 zucchini, cut into 1/2-inch thick rounds
  • 1 red onion, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 8 oz cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Instructions:

  1. If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent burning.
  2. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Thread the vegetables onto the skewers, alternating between different types of vegetables. Brush the skewers with the olive oil mixture.
  5. Grill the skewers for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and lightly charred.
  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and serve immediately. Enjoy!
